Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour Since 2003

What began as a humble kitchen passion in the lanes of Kalaburagi has grown into the region's most beloved biryani destination. Every pot we cook is a tribute to the Nawabi culinary tradition — unhurried, aromatic, and deeply soulful.

We source our spices directly from the Gulbarga spice markets, layer long-grain Basmati with hand-ground masalas, and seal every handi with dough before the final dum. No shortcuts. Ever.

What Sets Us Apart

The Safron Difference

🌾

Premium Basmati

Only aged Basmati from the foothills of the Himalayas, selected for its aroma, length, and the way each grain stays proudly separate after cooking.

🥩

Halal Certified Meat

Farm-sourced, halal-certified mutton and chicken, marinated overnight in yogurt and hand-ground spices for maximum depth of flavour.

🫧

The Dum Process

We seal our handis with wheat dough and cook on a slow charcoal flame for 45 minutes. The steam does the rest — creating biryani you simply cannot rush.

🌸

Kesar & Rose Water

A generous pinch of genuine Kashmiri saffron and a splash of distilled rose water are layered on every pot before sealing — our signature aroma.

🍚

Raita & Salan

Every biryani comes with house-made boondi raita and our fiery mirchi ka salan. Because biryani without accompaniments is a symphony without its orchestra.

🎁

Bulk & Event Orders

Weddings, corporate events, dawats — we cater with the same quality and care as we do in-house. DM or call to plan your occasion with us.
